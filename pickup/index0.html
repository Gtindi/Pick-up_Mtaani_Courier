ABOUT US


A well-integrated system should be implemented to ensure the efficient and seamless operation of courier services. Other Kenyan transport sectors, have recently implemented systems to handle all data and functions. PICK- UP MTAANI is a system meant to function with both small and major courier businesses. It offers all of the necessary features and capabilities to manage all of the operations associated with a courier service business.
